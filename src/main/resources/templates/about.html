About Us

Connecting CUET students through a trusted campus marketplace for academic resources, technology, and essential items.

OUR MISSION

To create a sustainable and accessible marketplace that empowers CUET students to maximize the value of their academic and personal resources through peer-to-peer transactions.

We believe in fostering a collaborative community where students can easily buy, sell, rent, and lend items, reducing waste and promoting economic sustainability within our campus ecosystem.

OUR VISION

To become the premier digital hub for CUET's student community, where every academic need can be met through peer connections and collaborative sharing.

We envision a future where students seamlessly access textbooks, lab equipment, tech gadgets, and hostel essentials through our platform, creating a self-sustaining ecosystem of resource sharing.

Our goal is to eliminate barriers to academic success by making essential items affordable and accessible to every CUET student.
